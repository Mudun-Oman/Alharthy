About Us

AL Harthy Investments LLC

Al Harthy Investments LLC, established in 1997 and based in Muscat, Oman, is a diversified business group with operations across the region. The company focuses on key sectors including information technology, real estate development, media, general trading, and business process outsourcing (BPO) for the banking and finance sector. With a commitment to innovation, integrity, and excellence, Al Harthy Investments delivers integrated solutions through its specialized subsidiaries. Backed by experienced leadership and a client-focused approach, the group is a trusted partner in driving sustainable business growth across the region.
