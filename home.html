About AHI

Al-Harthy Investments is a diversified holding company with strategic investments across high-growth sectors including Real Estate Development, Business Process Outsourcing, IT & Technology Solutions, Out-of-Home Media, and Shipping & Logistics. As a visionary investment leader, we combine deep sector expertise with market intelligence to identify and scale high-potential opportunities. Our real estate division delivers premium commercial and residential developments that redefine urban landscapes. Through our business outsourcing services, we enable operational excellence for global enterprises, while our technology arm drives digital transformation through innovative IT solutions. Our media portfolio connects brands with audiences through strategic OOH placements, and our shipping & logistics operations facilitate seamless global trade.

Guided by principles of innovation, sustainability, and value creation, we partner with investors and industry leaders to build future-ready businesses that deliver lasting impact and superior returns across all our verticals
